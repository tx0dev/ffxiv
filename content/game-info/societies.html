Allied Society (aka Beast Tribes)

They are Daily quests that require the players to perform tasks requested by various allied societies. You get "society token", EXP, gil, etc and repuation.

  • Max daily quests: 12
  • Max quests per society: 3
  • Reset: Daily at server reset (1500 GMT, or 1000 EST)
